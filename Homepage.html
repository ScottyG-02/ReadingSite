Reading shelf

Modern literacy is no longer about can read or cannot read. If you are in a first or second world country, most, if not everyone you know can read. The modern issue is what you choose to read. Do you choose to read books that uplift your intellect? Or distract you from the important issues of the day, or that which has the potential to better your being. This page is dedicated to books I love that have changed the way I think, and can potentially change the way you think.
